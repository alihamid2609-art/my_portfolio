Platform Flow

End-to-End Workflow Architecture

This architecture represents the full operational flow I build: collaboration to approvals, communication to reporting, and site-level execution to centralized oversight.

1. Teams-Style Collaboration Layer

Users create and discuss workflow items with mentions, ownership visibility, and contextual notes.

2. Workflow Engine

State transitions enforce process rules, assignment logic, and stage progression controls.

3. Document Approval Chain

Reviewer routing, deadline windows, and escalation policies manage document approvals end-to-end.

4. Email Integration Hub

Event-driven communication sends reminders, decisions, and SLA alerts to stakeholders.

5. Daily Reporting Pipeline

ETL processes aggregate workflow activity into daily operational and executive-level reporting.

6. Site Management Console

Site-specific process controls with centralized monitoring, trend analysis, and issue escalation.

Collaboration + Workflow + Approval + Communication + Reporting = reliable business execution at scale.
